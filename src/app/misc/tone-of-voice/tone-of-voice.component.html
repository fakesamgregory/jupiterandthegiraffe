Tone of Voice

Simply put, tone of voice is the personality of anything your company says. This can range for excitable to serious. Jupiter and the Giraffes tone of voice, for instance, is casual, quirky and personable (now you can't unsee it, right?)

We help you understand your tone on voice during your Strategy workshop. From this we outline areas in which your tone of voice can shine.

Tone of voice is not always something that is said or written. Tone should be considered in all aspects of your brand down to even the typography we choose. A very powerful tool.
