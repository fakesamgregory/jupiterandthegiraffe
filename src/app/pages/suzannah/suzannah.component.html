Your story

I started my professional career as a copywriter for the digital department at Vodafone UK on a graduate marketing scheme. From there I went into front-end development for a short while before I got my first full-time position as a developer at AND Digital (AND) where I fell in love with design. After carving out a design role at AND I was introduce to Samuel and we began doing side projects for Jupiter and the Giraffe together.

After successfully delivering several projects together we decided to go full-time with Jupiter and the Giraffe.
