CMS

Short for 'Content Managment System', CMS is a piece of software that manages the creation and modification of digital content and allows for the separation of content and presentation in a web application. CMS' allow for many users with varying permissions to manage this content.

Our CMS of choice is Wordpress. Wordpress is used by over 28% of all websites on the internet, and by 59% of all websites using a known content management system, followed by Joomla and Drupal.
