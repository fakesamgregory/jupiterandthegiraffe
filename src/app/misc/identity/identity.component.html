Brand Identity

Not to be confused with our "Identity" service, your brand identity (or logo) is usually the first thing people see. It's the most iconic part of your brand so shouldn't be understated.

Your identity can take many forms. An Icon, typeface or even both! Without an identity that has meaning your brands message is totally lost. It's a shortcut to your values, messaging and, of course, your identity.
